About

I am a researcher and designer working at the intersection of AI, learning sciences, and responsible innovation. My projects explore how people make decisions with algorithmic support, especially when the stakes are high and the context is complex.

I lead collaborative teams across academia and industry, translating evidence-based insights into real-world tools. My approach emphasizes transparency, inclusivity, and measurable impact.

Current roles

  • Principal Investigator, Human-AI Futures Lab
  • Visiting Scholar, MIT Media Lab
  • Advisor, Responsible AI Working Group

Research focus

My research agenda centers on equitable, interpretable, and emotionally supportive AI systems.

Explainable Interfaces

Building interfaces that reveal model confidence, data provenance, and uncertainty without overwhelming users.

Collaborative Decision-Making

Designing workflows where humans and AI negotiate, challenge, and refine each other’s perspectives.

Ethical Co-Design

Partnering with communities to embed values, fairness, and accountability into systems from the start.

Featured project

Guided reflection for AI-assisted learning

A mixed-methods study with educators and students to prototype reflective prompts that improve comprehension when learners use AI tutoring tools.
